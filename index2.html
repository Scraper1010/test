Monstera Deliciosa

Monstera deliciosa

The Monstera Deliciosa, also known as the Swiss Cheese Plant, is a stunning tropical houseplant beloved for its large, glossy leaves with distinctive splits and holes. Native to Central America, this climbing plant brings a touch of jungle elegance to any indoor space.

Pro Care Tips

  • Provide a moss pole or trellis for climbing support
  • Wipe leaves regularly to keep them glossy and dust-free
  • Fenestrations (holes) develop as the plant matures
  • Propagate easily through stem cuttings with aerial roots
  • Rotate weekly for even growth and light exposure
  • Repot every 2-3 years or when rootbound
